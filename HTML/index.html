Dice Game

Instructions:

  • Select how many players there are
  • On your turn, select roll the dice
  • If your dice roll lands on 1, 2, 3, 4 or 5 then this will be added to your score
  • If your dice roll lands on 6 then your score resets
  • If there are two players then player two will have their go
  • The first person to reach 21 wins
